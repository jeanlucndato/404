network-Architecture

–

(Intro)

                                              As the **Network Architect**, my primary challenge was the end-to-end deployment of a new large-scale infrastructure. This required designing a robust, highly-available, and secure network (L2/L3) capable of handling high-density traffic across multiple geographical points. I managed the entire lifecycle, from conceptualizing the topology (SD-WAN/MPLS) to the configuration and final implementation, ensuring zero downtime and optimal performance.            
               
                 
                   
                     
                      Client                       Espoir Group with Konnect (Multi-Site)                    
                 
                 
                   
                     
                      Sector                       Réseaux de Télécommunications & Cloud                    
                 
                 
                   
                     
                      Services                       SD-WAN Deployment, BGP/OSPF Routing, VXLAN/EVPN, Network Hardening                    
                 
                 
                   
                     
                      Year                       2022                    
                 
               
             
             
               

                                      (Technical Scope)                                  

             
             
               
                  Phase 1: High-Level Design and Optimization. My role was to engineer a highly resilient network architecture for the Global Datacom client, focusing on replacing their legacy MPLS backbone. I conducted an in-depth analysis of latency and throughput requirements, defining the optimal topology for SD-WAN deployment. This phase included flow modeling, platform selection, and establishing internal and external routing protocols (iBGP/eBGP) to ensure fast convergence and traffic efficiency.                
             
             
               
                  Phase 2: Advanced Deployment and Validation. Subsequently, I led the implementation of Data Center virtualization using VXLAN/EVPN for Layer 2 segmentation, critical for the hybrid Cloud environment. The configuration was fully automated using Infrastructure as Code (IaC) tools to ensure compliance and reproducibility. The final outcome is an agile, secured (Network Hardening) infrastructure capable of supporting exponential growth without compromising service quality or network resilience.
