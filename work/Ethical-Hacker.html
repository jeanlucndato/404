(Pentest Process)

The engagement begins with rigorous **Reconnaissance and Information Gathering** (passive and active scanning) to map the attack surface. This is followed by **Vulnerability Analysis** using automated tools and deep manual testing to identify configuration flaws, unpatched systems, and logical errors, especially against the **OWASP Top 10** for web applications. The goal of this phase is to create a prioritized list of weaknesses, focusing on those that pose the highest risk to the client's critical assets. We ensure all testing complies with the defined scope and **Rules of Engagement (ROE)**.
The next critical step is **Exploitation and Post-Exploitation**, where we safely demonstrate the impact of discovered flaws, aiming for system compromise or sensitive data access. Unlike automated scanning, we focus on **chaining multiple low-risk vulnerabilities** to achieve a high-impact result. The process concludes with detailed **Reporting and Remediation Guidance**. Every finding is clearly documented, providing a risk rating (CVSS score), proof-of-concept, and **specific, actionable fixes** to ensure the client can efficiently harden their environment and eliminate the identified security gaps.
